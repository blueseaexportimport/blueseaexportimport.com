The BLUE SEA EXPORT IMPORT S.R.L. Commission contract Procedure

We only buy your product for clients at their demand ( we don't store products if not requested by client company )

Step 1 You can read the commision contract to understand it
https://www.docdroid.net/eqpwaTm/commision-contract-the-principal-reads-fills-his-part-of-the-contract-and-sends-back-to-blue-sea-export-import-srl-email-pdf
" 5.2 The client has the right to inspect, test the quality of the goods described in Appendix no. 1 at the time of delivery and to reject them if they are not in accordance with the contractual clauses."

Step 2 After we verify your documents and you check our documents, we get exclusive rights agreement and you also sign the declaration of compliance if you wish to become our supplier

Step 3 We contact the client companies or they view your catalog and public documents you sent us on www.blueseaexportimport.com or social media ---> to show that product(s) is safe, respects environmental, human and animal rights legislation, that it's transparent and traceable and that your an authorised producer / transporter or other service provider ( client company buys what it wants, could be another product from another supplier )

Step 4 You send sample(s) to client(s) companies ( we pay you sample and transport cost, the company client will pay for it when it requests sample )

Step 5 If the client company likes the product(s), the client signs a commission contract with BLUE SEA EXPORT IMPORT S.R.L. ( we send you a copy also to read )

Step 6 If we all agree, the client company(s) sends payment in advance 100% to BLUE SEA EXPORT IMPORT S.R.L. after the invoice is generated.

Step 7 BLUE SEA EXPORT IMPORT S.R.L. send you the producer / transporter / logistics or anyone how is part of the supply chain a copy of bank statement proof that payment is in our account

Step 8 You ship product to each client depending on demand
( example
--- client A 3 tones small factory in country x
--- client B 10 tones supermarket chain in country y
--- client C 50 tones big processing factory in country z )

Step 9 Client(s) inspect the product(s) if according to Appendix I of commission contract and after client signs the receipt we send you the full payment to your bank account or if product not ok and not according to contract it is send to you back at your own cost or compost centers or recycling center.
